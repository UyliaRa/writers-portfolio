My next books

I don't like to tell about something I've not done yet. But my readers sometimes ask me what books I am going to write in the nearest time. Ok, I can announce that the second part of "The Star Courier" is already preparing for publishing. And there are some themes I'm working on at this time.

Ukrainian history

The goal of this story is to show children that the history of our country is not boring dusted books. It is a breathtaking adventures, a difficult and dramatic path which our ancestry had been going to make our country strong and independent.

Propaganda

At the beginning of the war, my eldest son Leo asked me: "Mom, how did it happen that the russian people suddenly turned into a monster?" Then I realized that as a children's writer I have a duty to explain to children how propaganda works.

Marketing

As a branding and marketing specialist, I can tell children a lot about how this area is organized from the inside. I came up with a fairy-tale character who will teach young readers to come up with their own marketing strategies and successfully sell goods and services in the future.
