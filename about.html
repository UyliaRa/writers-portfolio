About me

I was born on February 5, 1991, in Bila Tserkva (Kyiv region). I have dreamed of journalism since childhood, at the age of 12, I started to publish my articles in newspapers and magazines. In 2012 I graduated from the Institute of Journalism of Kyiv National University. In general, I had been working in journalism for more than 15 years.
Since 2015, I have been working as a professional idea generator - the creative director of a branding studio.
But my main position in life is a manager of overcoming age crises, a specialist in working with protests against vegetable stew and cleaning toys. In short - mom. This is my most difficult job, but it is the most inspiring. I have two sons: 8-year-old Leo and 2-year-old Plato. And in August 2022, our family is waiting for the appearance of the third son.
